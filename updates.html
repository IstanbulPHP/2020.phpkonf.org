Important PHPKonf 2020 Updates

We will continue to provide updates on this page as needed leading up to the event.

UPDATE AS OF NOVEMBER 29, 2020

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic we have transformed PHPKonf Istanbul PHP Conference 2020 to an online event. On December 12th we will offer a 1-day, single-track event free of charge for all attendees! So mark your calendar for Saturday, December 12th and we hope to virtually meet you at the online edition of PHPKonf!

İstanbul PHP

UPDATE AS OF MARCH 11, 2020

Over the past weeks, we have been closely monitoring global developments around the Coronavirus(COVID-19) outbreak. Unfortunately, today, Turkey's Health Minister confirmed the first case of coronavirus at the country.

The security and safety of our attendees, speakers and sponsors will always be our top priority. After quick and careful consideration we have made the difficult decision to postpone PHPKonf 2020. We understand the inconvenience this may cause but we will do it properly when the time is right and everyone feels safe and comfortable.

We are planning to host PHPKonf 2020 in the late summer. We hope you are able to join us and will provide an update with the new event dates as soon as possible. Please continue to follow our Twitter and Kommunity pages as we announce more updates about conference.

We would like to thank all those involved in organising PHPKonf: our delegates, our speakers, our sponsors, as well as the team, for your continued participation and support. All purchased conference passes will remain valid for the event. We look forward to welcoming you in Istanbul. We will honor full refunds for those that need this. For more information, please, reach out to mail@istanbulphp.org.

Our sympathies are with those affected during this difficult time.

İstanbul PHP
