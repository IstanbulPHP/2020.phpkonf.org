About PHPKonf

With over 600 delegates, speakers, and sponsors, PHPKonf İstanbul PHP conference aims to deliver fantastic up-to-date content about PHP and related web technologies in a comfortable and professional setting. There are countless networking opportunities to engage with international speakers and delegates, which makes the event one you won't want to miss.
